HOW TO USE THIS TOOL
CIPHER DAILY helps you find value bets by comparing your statistical model against the bookmaker's implied probability. It tells you when the odds are in your favour — and when to walk away.
🎾 TENNIS INPUTS
Serve Win % — How often a player wins their service games. Find this on ATP/WTA stats pages or flashscore.com. Typical range: 60–75%.
Return Win % — How often they win when returning. Typical range: 30–45%. Higher = better returner.
Surface — Clay slows the game (helps returners), Grass speeds it up (helps servers). Always match the actual surface.
Format — Best of 3 (most ATP/WTA) or Best of 5 (Grand Slams). Bo5 reduces variance, favourites win more often.
🏀 BASKETBALL INPUTS
Offensive Rating — Points scored per 100 possessions. Find on basketball-reference.com or NBA.com. Typical NBA range: 105–120.
Defensive Rating — Points allowed per 100 possessions. Lower = better defence.
Home Court — Home teams historically win ~60% of games. Always factor this in.
📊 READING THE RESULT
MODEL PROB — What our model thinks the real win probability is.
EDGE % — How much better your odds are vs the bookie. Positive = value bet. Negative = bookie has the edge.
EV — Expected Value per unit staked. +0.05 means you gain 5 units per 100 staked long-term.

BET IT — Edge >5% and positive EV. Strong signal.
MARGINAL — Small positive edge. Only bet if your stats are solid.
SKIP — No edge. The bookie's price is fair or against you. Don't force it.
⚠ This tool is a mathematical model — not a crystal ball. Results depend on the quality of stats you enter. Always use recent form data (last 10–15 matches). Bet responsibly.
