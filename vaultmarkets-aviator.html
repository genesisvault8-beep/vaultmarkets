// HOW TO USE AVIATOR SIGNAL
✈️ WHAT IT DOES
Aviator Signal analyses the last 5 crash rounds to detect patterns — streaks of low or high multipliers, momentum shifts, and volatility. It then suggests a cashout target for the next round based on that pattern.
📥 HOW TO INPUT
Enter the last 5 crash multipliers from your Aviator game into R1–R5 (R1 = oldest, R5 = most recent).
Minimum 3 rounds required to generate a signal. More rounds = better pattern detection.
Pills show LOW (under 1.5x), MID (1.5–3x), or HIGH (above 3x) for each round.
📊 READING THE SIGNAL
HOLD HIGHER — Pattern suggests next round may go higher. Target the suggested multiplier.
CASH EARLY — Pattern suggests correction incoming. Take small wins and protect bankroll.
PLAY SAFE — Session is volatile or neutral. No strong signal — conservative exit recommended.

Confidence — HIGH means the pattern is clear. LOW means the data is mixed — use extra caution.
Volatility — LOW means rounds are consistent. HIGH means unpredictable swings.
⚠ Aviator is a provably fair RNG game — past rounds have no mathematical influence on future rounds. This tool identifies patterns for strategic cashout planning only. It does not predict outcomes. Always set a stop-loss and never chase losses.
